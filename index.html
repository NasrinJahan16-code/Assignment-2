Run an Extra Mile easily

The run-walk method, or walk-run method, is simply a way of training that involves intervals of running mixed with intervals of walking. Over time, the running intervals get longer, and the walking intervals get shorter until you are finally running a full-time or distance without walking in between.

BMI Calculator

BMI (body mass index) is a measure for adults to check what category their height and weight puts them in underweight, healthy, or overweight.
  The calculator will give you an idea of how your weight compares to common values.BMI is calculated
as your weight divided by the square of your height or BMI = weight/height2.
